## **1. Goal**

Build a modern, simple, senior-friendly website for a 60-year-old Hasya Yoga teacher to:

* Showcase Hasya Yoga sessions
* Display Zoom schedules
* Showcase photo & video gallery
* Sell wellness products using QR payments + WhatsApp orders
* Connect to Instagram/Facebook content
* Allow easy admin updates (products, schedules, gallery images)
* Host completely on Vercel
* Use Firebase Firestore + Storage as backend (free tier)
